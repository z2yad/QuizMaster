Frequently Asked Questions

Is QuizMaster completely free to use?

Yes, QuizMaster is completely free to use! We believe in making knowledge accessible to everyone without any cost barriers.

Can I create my own quizzes on QuizMaster?

Currently, we're focused on providing curated content from reliable sources. However, we're working on a feature that will allow users to create and share their own quizzes in the future!

How often is new content added?

We add new quizzes and questions every week across various categories. Our content team works continuously to expand our knowledge database.

Is there a mobile app available?

Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. We're also developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, coming soon!
